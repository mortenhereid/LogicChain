## Active Retrieval Interface

This tool is designed to bridge the gap between "reading about" a subject and actually mastering it. Unlike standard quiz apps that focus on easy wins, this interface uses **high-difficulty discrimination** to force your brain to work for the answer—triggering the specific cognitive processes required for long-term retention.

### Why it works:
Most learning tools fail because they are too easy. This tool follows the science of **Desirable Difficulty**. By presenting options that require careful nuance to distinguish, it forces you to actively process the material rather than just recognize a pattern.

### The Three-Layer Mastery Loop:
When you answer a question, the tool provides a "debug-style" breakdown across three critical levels of understanding:

1.  **The Logic:** A concise rationale for why the answer is correct, immediately correcting your mental model.
2.  **The Depth:** A detailed academic "deep dive" for context, grounding the fact in the broader subject framework.
3.  **The Practical:** A concrete, real-world example that translates abstract theory into a scenario you can actually use.

### Value-Driven Design:
* **Zero-Friction Flow:** Integrated tooltips provide instant definitions for technical jargon, keeping you in a state of focus without needing to switch tabs or search for meanings.
* **Active Discrimination:** Questions are engineered to be challenging, forcing the mind to evaluate and compare similar concepts—the single most effective way to lock in new knowledge.
* **Modular Learning:** Effortlessly switch between diverse subject sets like ITIL 4 or Learning Science while maintaining the same rigorous feedback loop.
